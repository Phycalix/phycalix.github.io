Meet Our Founder & CEO

Victor Caxala

Victor Caxala is a biochemist, entrepreneur, and visionary with a passion for microalgae biotechnology. As the founder and CEO of Phycalix, he combines science, design, and sustainability to create innovative products that have a positive impact on people and the planet. His first product, the microalgae lamp PHYCOlumina, embodies his approach: aesthetic, functional, and future-oriented. With Phycalix, he aims to redefine microalgae as a key technology for nutrition, medicine, and environmental solutions.
